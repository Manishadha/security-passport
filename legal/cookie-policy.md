## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies and similar technologies are used in connection with the SecurityPassport website and application.

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device when visiting a website.

They allow websites to recognize devices and store certain information.

## 3. Cookies We Use

The platform may use the following types of cookies:

Essential cookies  
These cookies are necessary for the operation of the website and authentication sessions.

Session cookies  
Temporary cookies used to maintain login sessions.

Security cookies  
Used to detect and prevent unauthorized access.

## 4. Cookies We Do Not Use

The service currently does not use advertising or behavioral tracking cookies.

If analytics services are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated.

## 5. Managing Cookies

Users can control cookies through browser settings.

Disabling cookies may affect certain functionality of the platform.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically.

The latest version will always be available on the website.
